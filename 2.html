Play art and creativity games

You can let your spirit soar with each one of the inspiring online games that you’ll find in this category. If you’ve ever wanted to try your hand at painting a masterpiece, or at least a virtual one, here’s your chance. You can also dive into plenty of other projects that will give your creativity and other talents a good workout.

You can create sketches, drawings, and paintings with brushes, crayons, markers, and pencils. In some games, you can make them completely from scratch whereas others feature coloring books. Can you stay within the lines while you fill black and white drawings with tons of color? Should a picture of an adorable doggy be painted blue or even purple? You’ll get to decide! You might even find yourself re-painting a famous work of art by one of history’s greatest painters.

There are also games that feature origami and paper mache. You can fold pieces of virtual paper to create amazing cranes, pandas, and other animals. You’ll also get to create objects ranging from boats to trees and more. Other games in this category feature additional creative projects that will keep you busy for hours. Have fun while you cover a canvas with an amazing portrait when you’re not designing a sculpture or a timeless masterpiece with all of these free online games.
